Elsewhere: @zineteamlive + zineteamlive@gmail.com

Zine Team Live is an independent publishing initiative in Jamaica Plain, Massachusetts. We make zines about experimental games, poetry, prose, photographs, and network culture. Our goal is to encourage as many people as possible to fearlessly produce work that is fun, sad, scary, and weird. Pins and stand-alone prints are tertiary to our offerings, but we willingly reserve space on our table for new ideas.
